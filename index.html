Sales System

The 7-Day
Sales Rhythm

Success follows a structured weekly cadence — not improvised hustle. Tap each day to expand the full activity list. Each day has a clear primary goal, defined activities, and measurable outputs.

1
Lead Generation + First Pitch Outbound
Primary Goal: Fill the pipeline
Tap to expand
  • Identify 25–30 new leads from Instagram followers, LinkedIn connections and engagers
  • Vet suitability and add qualified prospects to the Sales Spreadsheet
  • Send 15–20 first-touch pitches across LinkedIn, IG DM, or email
  • Organize all prospects by warm, hot, or nurture status
2
Follow-Ups + Social Engagement
Primary Goal: Convert silent viewers into conversations
Tap to expand
  • Touchpoint #2 to all who viewed but did not reply
  • Light social touches — IG Story likes, LinkedIn post reactions
  • Respond to all active conversations; log new warm leads
3
Value Add + Proof of Concept
Primary Goal: Move from "interested" to "motivated"
Tap to expand
  • Touchpoint #3 — published story examples, issue links, industry insights
  • Share example layouts to help prospects visualize their placement
  • Update spreadsheet with stage movement and notes
4
Heavy Follow-Up + Referral Outreach
Primary Goal: Push leads toward decision-making
Tap to expand
  • Touchpoint #4 to all engaged but uncommitted prospects
  • Reach out to past participants for referrals — highest close rate in the system
  • Offer referral fee or charitable donation option for legacy contacts
  • Book discovery calls for Days 5 and 6
5
Conversion Calls
Primary Goal: Close warm and hot leads
Tap to expand
  • Conduct 10–15 minute discovery Zooms or calls
  • Present Snapshot, QuickTake, Journey Story, or Special Edition options
  • Close same-day where possible; send recap emails and payment links
6
Final Follow-Up + Objection Handling
Primary Goal: Convert remaining warm leads before they cool
Tap to expand
  • Touchpoint #5 or #6 depending on cycle stage
  • "I have one space remaining before layout — shall I hold it for you?"
  • Address objections: timing, budget, decision partners
  • Offer 48-hour space hold, referral fee, or charity donation when appropriate
7
Pipeline Hygiene + Weekly Reset
Primary Goal: Set up a strong Monday launch
Tap to expand
  • Review and update all lead statuses: New / Warm / Hot / Nurture / Closed-Won
  • Review IG and LinkedIn for emerging leads from the week
  • Export nurture leads into the long-term monthly flow
  • Refresh message templates for the coming week

Daily Work Block

Three hours.
Structured for results.

The core daily work block is designed for maximum outreach impact in a focused, sequenced session. Each block feeds directly into the 7-Day Rhythm. Select a time block below to see the full breakdown.

0:00–0:30
Instagram · Accounts Management
Instagram Account Management
Review all new notifications across @portfolio.yvr, @folio.yvr, and @vancouvervices. Identify new followers and vet for suitability — entrepreneurs, founders, creatives, luxury lifestyle, hospitality, wellness, finance. For suitable new followers, send a brief welcome and introduce your role immediately.
  • Log all warm leads into the Sales Spreadsheet with status immediately
  • Engage intentionally — like Stories of those who viewed yesterday's pitches
  • Respond to all replies from pitches earlier in the week
  • Target: 5–10 new qualified leads identified and messaged
0:30–0:45
Instagram · Stories Monitoring
Stories Monitoring
Watch Stories from warm prospect accounts. Look specifically for signals — launches, announcements, new services or expansions, events, professional wins. Anything that signals they may benefit from coverage or storytelling is an opening for outreach.
  • Screen-snap suitable leads and email captures to yourself with next-step notes
  • Add warm prospects from this session to the Sales Spreadsheet immediately
  • Engagement at this stage keeps you top-of-mind before a pitch lands
0:45–1:45
LinkedIn · Business Development
LinkedIn Business Development
Review all engagement on ELL Comms posts and reshared content. Pitch individuals who engaged with our LinkedIn posts. Systematically open each new connection profile — this signals renewed interest and invites profile views back. Pitch those who meet target criteria.
  • Pitch all individuals who engaged with our content that day
  • When encountering past-issue contacts: reintroduce yourself + offer referral program
  • Log all warm LinkedIn leads under "Warm / Legacy Leads"
  • Target: 10–15 new pitches sent in this block
1:45–2:15
Email · Cold Outreach
Cold Email Campaign
Send 15 targeted cold emails sourced from local publication lists where HS has already sent an introductory Flodesk message through bulk email. These are pre-warmed contacts — not cold in the traditional sense. After each email, set a follow-up reminder for three days later.
  • Source only from the pre-approved local publication lists
  • Set every follow-up reminder before moving to the next email
  • Target: 15 emails sent, 15 follow-up reminders set
2:15–3:00
All Channels · Design & Respond
Design & Respond Session
Write thoughtful, tailored replies to warm leads from Instagram, LinkedIn, and email. Adjust pitch templates for industry relevance and personality. Create short pitch variations — one-paragraph versions, bullet summaries, or one-sheet attachments.
  • Every active conversation must be logged before this block ends
  • Prepare the next day's outbound message queue
  • Craft at least 2 new pitch variations for the week ahead
